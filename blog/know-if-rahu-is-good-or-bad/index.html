Know if Rahu is benefic or malefic

  • Home
  • /

Astrology 2nd January

This article is about knowing if Rahu is benefic or malefic without having knowledge of Jyotish. This article will give you an initial check to know if Rahu is beneficial for you or not. To know more, please connect with a good astrologer. Let's dive in

Disclaimer: Traits mentioned here are only general in nature and may vary depending upon individual birth chart. To now more, book a consultation

1.) Rahu is connected to Grandfather, check the lifestyle of your grandfather and his respect in society. This way you may know a scale on which your Rahi is tilting.

2.) Rahu represents In-laws family; check how cordial your relations are with your In-laws

3.) Rahu represents your temper and kitchen in your house. Check if you have or initiate quarrels or loose your temper in kitchen

4.) Rahu represents toilet in your home. Check how clean or managed toilets are, in your home.

5.) Malefic Rahu represents rusted scrap items, unused old electronic equipments, old coins (which are discontinued). If you collect any of the items mentioned above; Your rahu may be malefic

6.) If you have bipolar disorder or anger issues, you may have malefic rahu aspecting moon or mars
