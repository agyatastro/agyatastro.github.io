What is Premium Consultation

Premium Consultation is consultation method, where people who wants to know their chart in depth, wish to take guidance and any query around the birth chart

How to Book

To book this consulation, first step is to make payment using the scanner on this page (for Indian Clients Only)

For International Clients, please contact consult@agyat.one

Once the payment is successfully done, note down the Payment/Transaction ID. Now next step is to fill the form using the button below

Premium Consultation

Once form is filled you may get an email for same within 3 working days.

Schedule Time

In this consultation methods, there are two callbacks are scheduled. First will be a telephonic conversation, where the team will analyse your chart and match it with past event predictions. If past event predictions does not match than this consultation is automatically be put for Refund and you will advised to go for BTR (Birth Time Rectification) or book again with correct birth details

Once Consultation is booked, you will get a confirmation email with time slot for first round. If analysis for past event does not match, this consultation is automatically be put for Refund. At this stage you will also have a say if you wish to cancel and proceed for refund

Refund requests will not be entertained after first round

A Second round of consultation (via Microsoft Team or Zoom Meetings) will be booked, if you are willing. Once confirmed by you a confirmation email with schedule will be shared

Mode of Consultation

This consultation in first round is given maximum 15 minutes of times and happens over a phone call. Second round will be scheduled via Microsoft Team or Zoom Meetings and given maximum 30 minutes of time

Refund and Cancellation

Refund is allowed after first round, if you are not willing to go ahead. Refund is automatically be done if astrologer on call is not convinced with past event predictions

Refund requests are not entertained once second round is booked and confirmed
