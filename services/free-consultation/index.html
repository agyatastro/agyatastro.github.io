What is a Free Vedic Astrology Consultation?

Our Free Consultation is a limited-access service designed for individuals seeking expert astrological advice on a single, pressing concern. This complimentary session allows you to ask one specific question—whether regarding career growth, marriage compatibility, or financial prospects—to receive a professional Vedic birth chart analysis at no cost.

Please Note: Due to high demand, submitting the form does not guarantee a selection. Our team chooses lucky participants for these complimentary astrology readings based on schedule availability.

How to Apply for Your Free Session

Free Consultation

Upon submitting your application for a free Vedic astrology session, our team will review your query. If selected, you will receive a follow-up email with further instructions on how to proceed.

Consultation Scheduling & Availability

If your request for a complimentary astrology reading is accepted, you will receive an email detailing your specific appointment time. Please note that these exclusive timeslots are final; to ensure we can help as many seekers as possible, we cannot accommodate changes or rescheduling requests for free sessions.

Format of the Free Consultation

This single-query consultation is a focused, 10-minute session designed to provide quick astrological clarity. You will receive an email or call from our expert team at your fixed appointment time. We kindly ask that you be ready and available, as missed sessions cannot be rescheduled.

To respect the expert's time and other applicants, please ensure you are available precisely at your assigned timeslot.
