Astrology 1st December, 2024

This story is not about prediction but basically to share some mysterious Ketu traits in all 12 house.

Note: Treat this as traits of Ketu not as prediction. Here we are only taking Ketu in isolation (without any conjunction) in this story. Outcome may differ in other cases (as per PAC)

Ketu in 1st House: Native with Ketu in 1st house will be quiet natured (not outspoken)(in some cases native is found to be introvert), but will very be good at dealing with the worldly affairs.

Once in every 36 years of cycle native will fail at worldly affairs and at the same time will be outspoken. Native having Ketu in first house thinks aloud before taking actions. If Ketu is alone in 1st house, native is possibly born away from paternal house.

Ketu in 2nd house also make native devoid of savings and native goes through phases of depressions in his/her lifetime. Ketu in 2nd house may also give authoritative positions.

Ketu in 3rd House: Ketu in 3rd house is generally not considered good. I have only one advice for Ketu in 3rd house is that; native should avoid arguments, litigations with brothers.

Ketu in 3rd house also make native fight unnecessary litigations/court cases. Ketu in 3rd house makes native go deep into law and find loopholes to subvert them (even when native is not a lawyer by profession)

Ketu in 3rd house, may also devoid native of expressing through writing. Native can not put his thoughts to words. Anger management is most necessary aspect in this placement.

Again, Once in 36 years of cycle, this ketu makes native very polite with brothers and humble.
