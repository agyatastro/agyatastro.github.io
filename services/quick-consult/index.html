What is Quick Consult

Quick consult is consultation method, where people who have a single query can avail this consultation method. This allows native to ask any one question (i.e marriage, career)

Even if your question does not fit into any specific category, this option can be availed

Schedule Time

Once Consultation is booked, you will get a confirmation email with open timeslot for schedule. you can choose any time-slot as per your convenience. Cancellation will be available at this stage.
