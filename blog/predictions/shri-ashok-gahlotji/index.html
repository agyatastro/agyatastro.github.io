Astrology 8th January, 2025

After turbulent infighting in Rajasthan Congress in last term and subsequent loss in recent legislative elections for Rajasthan, surrounded by controversies, Ex CM of Rajasthan Shri Ashok Gahlot ji is out of power now.

In this article we are going to look into his chart and with blessing of The God, going to forecast upcoming time for him.

Brief Introduction

Shri Ashok Gahlot ji is a member of the Indian National Congress (INC) party. He was actively engaged in social political work even as a student. During the East Bengali refugees crisis in 1971, he served in the refugee camps in the eastern states of India. It is there when former Prime Minister Indira Gandhi first identified his organizational skills during one of her visits to the refugee camps. Gehlot was later appointed the first State President of National Students' Union of India and successfully organized Congress's Student Wing in the state. He is long time politician and is known to be close to Gandhi Family. He eats before sunset and is a pure vegetarian and enjoys Satvik Meals, thereby making him a teetotaler.

Gehlot is known for his political acumen and most often, he is referred to as magician (jadugar) by his party colleagues. He holds significant hold in Rajasthan Congress, and served as Chief Minister for 3 terms. He is known to sideline his detractors to continue to have strong hold is Rajasthan Congress politics.

Brief Chart analysis

The chart displayed here is being analyzed by Nadi and other Jyotish Principles, hence chart displayed below has Jupiter as ascendant.

In his chart, it can be clearly seen that Jupiter and Moon are conjunct, blessing him with Gajkesari Yoga, a yoga which if fructify can give native a life without much hurdle. Moon and Jupiter also conjunct by degrees (within 10 degrees), which makes it to fructify.

Rahu in Capricorn and Ketu in Leo, makes him ambitious and through authority. Ketu in Aries (1st), Leo(5th) or in Capricorn(10th) gives native the authority or connections with authorities (Refer article Ketu through 12 houses). He has served as Chief Minister of Rajasthan for 3 terms, been minister in central government and considered as close aide for Gandhi Family.

Future Prediction (Major)

Here are few major prediction for Shri Ashok Gahlot ji for next few years.

Note: We are only giving importance to major events that may happen in his life in upcoming years

  • In upcoming years, Shri Ashok Gahlotji, would be seen as a great orator. He may be employed as star campaigner or given a position in other states election (apart from Rajasthan).

  • Shri Ashok Gahlot ji may come in limelight for his relations with his siblings (mostly in positive manner). He may influence people by his writings and communication

  • Shri Ashok Gahlot ji, may find him engulfed in controversies, arising from foreign land (outside of Rajasthan) or foreign person. He may be targeted for spending done in his tenure as chief minister of Rajasthan.

  • Shri Ashok Gahlot ji, may get entangled in legal issues, which may intensify in 2026 and 2027.

  • Shri Ashok Gahlot ji, may be given a authoritative post in year 2026.

  • There may be attempt to defame Shri Ashok Gahlot ji in year 2026 and 2027 with legal prosecution being initiated against him.

  • After June 2027, Shri Ashok Gahlot ji, may suffer from Chronic diseases, either related to blood vessals, blood, skin, mouth related and it seems to last longer (more than 3 years).
